Crunchwrap Supreme

Equipment

  • frying pan
  • cutting board
  • chef's knife
  • Measuring spoons
  • skillet
  • measuring cups

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2 Tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 6 burrito-size flour tortillas
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 6 taco-size flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup Nacho cheese sauce
  • 6 tostada shells
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cups Mexican cheese blend

Directions

  1. First, make the meat mixture. Then drain the grease from the pan.
  2. Next, heat the tortillas in the microwave and the nacho cheese sauce.
  3. Assemble the crunch wrap by layering a tortilla with meat, nacho cheese, a tostada, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, and shredded cheese, and then top it with a tortilla.
  4. Fold up the edges of the tortilla and cook on each side for about a minute.
  5. Finally, serve immediately with toppings of your choice.
